History



TheDealsplace.com operates in the online booking market since 2008. We started as a small business working in an affiliate car rental program in Nairobi. Soon we realized that our customer’s needs exceeded just car booking in Nairobi. so we started to offer this service also in other Cities. We created a platform that would not only allow our clients to book a car, but to make this process as easy as possible allowing our clients to choose their most suitable car rental plan. Today TheDealsplacecom takes the leading positions in in the all country Internet booking market with a wide range of services as well as our service are now available in more than 6 cities . At the moment TheDealsplace.com has more than 55 employees each of whom is a professional in their own field. For number of years most of our employees have worked in various distribution companies in the capital city, and therefore have the right knowledge and experience to satisfy clients ever growing needs. We are constantly developing and we will certainly surprise our competitors by developing new IT projects in various fields of online tourism. We believe that our work brings positive benefits to our customers and a pleasure of working with us.



Philosophy


Our company's philosophy is to facilitate our customers to book car rental service anywhere in the world while our motto is – ‘’Providing quality service at an affordable price’’. Every day our analyst's tasks are to monitor prices plans of our co-operating agencies around the world as a result we can always guarantee very competitive prices and a high level of customer service. Our company has at least 3-4 Car Rental service providers in each destination. This allows for competition and higher customer care also providing healthier competition and better level of customer satisfaction.



Our company policy


Our company’s policy is this: we are with the customer during the booking process, before and after the car rental lease. Our clients can always feel safe as all issues related to reservations will be assisted by our specialists that will be there to help to solve any issue and provide extensive car rental experience. Our company’ s home page is designed so that our clients receive the maximum information about future car rental. Please note that we have an active call-center and all the questions you might have will be answered over the phone should you not find an answer on our home page. We offer and ask our customers to call us direct over our phone number where our operators will know all of the answers to all of your questions.
